The Nav Team templates and components Last updated April 2022

The Nav Team on NHS.UK (previously known as the Information Architecture team) have been working on updating page templates and components across the website to improve users' abilities to understand what content we have, how they can find it and what else we have to offer.

This prototype is a catalogue of the templates and components that contribute to a good information architecture. Where possible, guidance will be included explaining how to use these.

Guidance

  • Page templates

    The new page templates help users to navigate from a wide topic area, to more specific content

  • Components

    Components that show users to understand where they are in the site, what else is available and how they can get to it

Contribute to our GitHub issues

We are always looking for insight around how to improve our page templates and components.

If your team have used any of them, please find the information architecture issues on GitHub and share your findings.

Get in touch

For questions about how to design with the new templates and components, please contact Brie Whyatt on the nhs.uk slack, or email brieanna.whyatt1@nhs.net
